Annex A Conformance (Informative)

A.1 Current and Previous Versions

This edition has the version number 1.1 as part of its title. Version 1.0 was the first edition of the SWHID Specification as a Publicly Available Standard, and earlier editions of the specification were published by the Software Heritage. Differences between this edition and earlier ones are reported in the text; see also [1].

A.2 Obsolete features

Over the life of a standard, some older approaches can become obsolete and are dropped from subsequent editions, possibly with a replacement approach being provided. Such action involves deprecating those outdated features. This edition identifies all currently deprecated features.
